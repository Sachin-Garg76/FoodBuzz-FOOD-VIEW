#FoodBuzz – Reel-Based Food Ordering System

FoodBuzz is a reel-based food ordering system that transforms the way users discover and order food. Inspired by the short-video experience, users can browse engaging food reels, explore dishes visually, and place orders directly from the content they enjoy.

📖 About

Traditional food ordering platforms rely on static images and menus. FoodBuzz introduces a more interactive approach by allowing users to discover food through short, engaging reels, making the ordering experience faster, more enjoyable, and visually appealing.

✨ Key Features
🎥 Browse food through short reels
🍕 Discover trending and popular dishes
🔍 Explore food items with an interactive interface
🛒 Order food directly from reels
📱 Simple and user-friendly experience
🎯 Objective

The goal of FoodBuzz is to make food discovery more engaging by combining the entertainment of short-form videos with the convenience of online food ordering.

🚀 Future Scope
Personalized food recommendations
Restaurant profiles and menus
Secure online payments
Order tracking
User reviews and ratings
AI-powered food suggestions
